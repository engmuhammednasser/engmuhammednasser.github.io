Landing Page / Logistics Website

Ashhalan Logistics

A professional landing page for Ashhalan Logistics, specializing in parcel delivery, order fulfillment, and shipping solutions within Saudi Arabia. Built with Next.js using a fully custom interface, designed to clearly and quickly present the company's services, build trust, highlight speed and reliability, and facilitate easy contact for clients and businesses.

View Live Site

The Problem

The company needed a clear and direct Landing Page to introduce their logistics services professionally, present the company's value proposition, and demonstrate its capabilities in parcel transport, order delivery, and shipment management within Saudi Arabia. The primary goal was to convert visitors from browsers into potential clients who could easily get in touch or request a service.

The Solution

Developed a fully custom Landing Page using Next.js, with all sections and components built hardcoded from scratch without relying on any ready-made template. The page is structured starting with a strong Hero Section presenting the company's message, moving through services, features, and trust signals, culminating in a clear Call To Action for contact and service requests. The custom build provided full control over design, content layout, performance, and user experience, while maintaining a simple, fast interface suited to the nature of transport and logistics companies.

Key Features

  • Professional custom Landing Page design
  • Frontend built with Next.js
  • All sections and components built hardcoded from scratch
  • No ready-made templates used
  • Strong Hero Section with clear marketing message
  • Organized display of transport and delivery services
  • Highlights of key strengths: speed, trust, security, and reliability
  • Clear Call To Action for contact and service requests
  • Simple and straightforward user experience
  • Responsive design for mobile and tablet
  • Optimized page load speed and browsing experience
  • Content structured to drive conversions and attract leads
