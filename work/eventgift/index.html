Business Challenge

Business Challenge

EventGift needed a professional gifting platform that could serve multiple regional markets while keeping the shopping experience clear, trustworthy, and easy to browse. The platform needed to support product discovery, gifting categories, market-specific storefronts, corporate gifting use cases, and a responsive ecommerce experience across desktop and mobile.

Solution

A WordPress / WooCommerce ecommerce platform was built with structured product browsing, clear product presentation, market-specific storefront experiences, responsive frontend implementation, and a scalable backend setup that supports different markets under the same brand.
